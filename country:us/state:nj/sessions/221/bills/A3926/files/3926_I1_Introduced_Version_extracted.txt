Section 1:
A3926 ASSEMBLY, No. 3926 STATE OF NEW JERSEY 221st LEGISLATURE INTRODUCED MARCH 4, 2024 Sponsored by: Assemblyman  AL BARLAS District 40 (Bergen, Essex and Passaic) Assemblyman  CHRISTOPHER P. DEPHILLIPS District 40 (Bergen, Essex and Passaic) SYNOPSIS Makes $500,000 supplemental appropriation from Property Tax Relief Fund to Borough of North Caldwell to fund restoration of Walkers Family Home at Walkers Park and Walkers Park property. CURRENT VERSION OF TEXT As introduced. A Supplement to the annual appropriations act for the fiscal year ending June 30, 2024, P.L.2023, c.74. Be It Enacted by the Senate and the General Assembly of the State of New Jersey: 1.  In addition to the amounts appropriated under P.L.2023, c.74, the annual appropriations act for the fiscal year ending June 30, 2024, there is appropriated from the Property Tax Relief Fund the following amount for the purpose specified: 22 DEPARTMENT OF COMMUNITY AFFAIRS 70 Government, Direction, Management, and Control 75 State Subsidies and Financial Aid STATE AID 04-8030  Local Government Services ................................... $500,000 Total State Aid appropriation, State Subsidies and Financial Aid ...................... $500,000 State Aid: 04    Borough of North Caldwell  Restoration of the Walkers Family Home at Walkers Park and the Walkers Park property (PTRF)           ($500,000) 2.  This act shall take effect immediately. STATEMENT This bill appropriates an additional $500,000 from the Property Tax Relief Fund to the Borough of North Caldwell, Essex County, to fund the restoration of the Walkers family home at Walkers Park and the Walkers Park property.  Walkers Park is a 17-acre site acquired by North Caldwell in 2016.  The sponsor intends that the funds appropriated in the bill would be used for the restoration, and that the Borough of North Caldwell be able to use the restored home and property as it sees fit.
