STATEMENT

 

     This bill amends P.L.1948, c.110 and P.L.1989, c.261 to have the paid and unpaid leave provided under those laws include leave time in which an employee who is intended to become the parent of a child under a written agreement with a gestational carrier or otherwise provides care for the person whose pregnancy will result in the child during the persons pregnancy and recovery from childbirth.
