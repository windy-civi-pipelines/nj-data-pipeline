Section 1:
A2692 ASSEMBLY, No. 2692 STATE OF NEW JERSEY 221st LEGISLATURE PRE-FILED FOR INTRODUCTION IN THE 2024 SESSION Sponsored by: Assemblyman  JAY WEBBER District 26 (Morris and Passaic) SYNOPSIS Directs board of trustees of county college to post information on the compensation paid to college president on its website. CURRENT VERSION OF TEXT Introduced Pending Technical Review by Legislative Counsel. An Act concerning information on the compensation paid to county college presidents and supplementing chapter 64A of Title 18A of the New Jersey Statutes. Be It Enacted by the Senate and General Assembly of the State of New Jersey: 1.    The board of trustees of a county college shall post on its website: the employment contract between the board and the president of the college; and any payments, emoluments, or allowances provided to the president in addition to the salary and benefits paid under the contract. 2.    This act shall take effect immediately and shall be applicable to employment contracts entered into on or after the effective date of this act. STATEMENT This bill directs the board of trustees of a county college to post on its website: the employment contract between the board and the president of the college; and any payments, emoluments, or allowances provided to the president in addition to the salary and benefits paid under the contract.
