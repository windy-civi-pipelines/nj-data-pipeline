STACK District 33 (Hudson) Co-Sponsored by: Senator Burgess i SYNOPSIS Prohibits BPU approval of electric or gas public utility rate increases resulting in total increase to average residential customer bill in excess of two percent within five-year period. CURRENT VERSION OF TEXT As introduced. An Act concerning electric and gas public utility rates and supplementing Title 48 of the Revised Statutes. Be It Enacted by the Senate and General Assembly of the State of New Jersey: 1.    The Board of Public Utilities shall not approve electric or gas public utility rate increases that are projected to result in a total increase to an average residential customer bill in excess of two percent during any five-year period. 2.    This act shall take effect immediately. STATEMENT This bill prohibits the Board of Public Utilities from approving electric or gas public utility rate increases that are projected to result in a total increase to an average residential customer bill in excess of two percent during any five-year period. ================================================================================ Raw Text: S4372 SENATE, No. 4372 STATE OF NEW JERSEY 221st LEGISLATURE INTRODUCED MAY 15, 2025 Sponsored by: Senator  BRITNEE N.
