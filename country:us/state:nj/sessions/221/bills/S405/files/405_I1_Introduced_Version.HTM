STATEMENT

 

     This bill eliminates the age restriction governing the issuance of

permits to carry handguns to retired law enforcement officers.

     Currently, retired law enforcement officers may receive a permit to carry a handgun without having to establish justifiable need until they have attained the age of 75. This bill eliminates that age limitation.
