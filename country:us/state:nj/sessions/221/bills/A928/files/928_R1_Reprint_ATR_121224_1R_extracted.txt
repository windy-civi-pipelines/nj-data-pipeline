Section 1:
A928 1R [First Reprint] ASSEMBLY, No. 928 STATE OF NEW JERSEY 221st LEGISLATURE PRE-FILED FOR INTRODUCTION IN THE 2024 SESSION Sponsored by: Assemblywoman  VERLINA REYNOLDS-JACKSON District 15 (Hunterdon and Mercer) Assemblyman  ANTHONY S. VERRELLI District 15 (Hunterdon and Mercer) SYNOPSIS Requires maintenance of roadways surrounding State capitol complex. CURRENT VERSION OF TEXT As reported by the Assembly Transportation and Independent Authorities Committee on December 12, 2024, with amendments. An Act requiring maintenance of the roadways that surround the State capitol complex and supplementing Title 27 of the Revised Statutes. Be It Enacted by the Senate and General Assembly of the State of New Jersey: 1.    The Department of Transportation shall maintain the roadways that surround the State capitol complex, including: West State Street from Calhoun Street to South Warren Street; West Lafayette Street from Barrack Street to South Warren Street; Barrack Street from West State Street to Memorial Drive; and Memorial Drive.  Maintenance of these roadways shall include 1[, but not be limited to,]1 road paving and snow plowing.  To assist in fulfilling the requirements of this section, the department may consult 1[,]1 or contract with any State agency, county, or municipality that is responsible for maintaining the adjoining portion of each of the roadways. 2.  This act shall take effect immediately.
