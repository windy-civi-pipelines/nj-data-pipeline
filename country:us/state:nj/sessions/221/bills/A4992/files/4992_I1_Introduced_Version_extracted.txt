Section 1:
A4992 ASSEMBLY, No. 4992 STATE OF NEW JERSEY 221st LEGISLATURE INTRODUCED OCTOBER 24, 2024 Sponsored by: Assemblyman  DAVID BAILEY, JR. District 3 (Cumberland, Gloucester and Salem) Assemblyman  ANTWAN L. MCCLELLAN District 1 (Atlantic, Cape May and Cumberland) Assemblyman  DAN HUTCHISON District 4 (Atlantic, Camden and Gloucester) Co-Sponsored by: Assemblyman Simonsen SYNOPSIS Provides project grant under Securing Our Childrens Future Fund for career and technical education expansion; appropriates $7.65 million. CURRENT VERSION OF TEXT As introduced. An Act concerning project grants under the Securing Our Childrens Future Fund for career and technical education expansion and making an appropriation. Be It Enacted by the Senate and General Assembly of the State of New Jersey: 1.  a.  There is appropriated from the Securing Our Childrens Future Fund, established pursuant to section 13 of P.L.2018, c.119, to the Department of Education the sum of $7,650,000 to provide a grant for career and technical education expansion in the States county vocational school districts.  The project grant shall be approved in the amount set forth in subsection b. of this section. b.  The total $7,650,000 amount appropriated pursuant to subsection a. of this section shall be awarded to the Cumberland County Technical Education Center in Cumberland County. 2.  The expenditure of sums appropriated pursuant to this act shall be subject to the provisions and conditions of the Securing Our Childrens Future Bond Act, P.L.2018, c.119. 3.  This act shall take effect immediately. STATEMENT This bill appropriates a total of $7.65 million from the Securing Our Childrens Future Fund.  The total amount is directed to the Cumberland County Technical Education Center in Cumberland County for career and technical education expansion.
