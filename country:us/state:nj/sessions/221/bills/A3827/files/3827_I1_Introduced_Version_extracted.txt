Section 1:
A3827 ASSEMBLY, No. 3827 STATE OF NEW JERSEY 221st LEGISLATURE INTRODUCED FEBRUARY 22, 2024 Sponsored by: Assemblywoman  VICTORIA A. FLYNN District 13 (Monmouth) Assemblyman  GERRY SCHARFENBERGER District 13 (Monmouth) Assemblywoman  DAWN FANTASIA District 24 (Morris, Sussex and Warren) Co-Sponsored by: Assemblymen Inganamort, Azzariti Jr. and Auth SYNOPSIS Prohibits institutions of higher education from requiring students to receive COVID-19 vaccination. CURRENT VERSION OF TEXT As introduced. An Act concerning COVID-19 vaccination requirement in higher education and supplementing Title 18A of the New Jersey Statutes. Be It Enacted by the Senate and General Assembly of the State of New Jersey: 1.    This act shall be known and may be cited as the COVID-19 Vaccine Requirement Prohibition Act for Higher Education. 2.    An institution of higher education shall not require a student to receive a vaccination against COVID-19, or present evidence of such vaccination, as a condition of: enrollment in the institution; attendance of courses taught at the institution; attendance of courses taught at the institution; or participation in any program sponsored by the institution. 3.    This act shall take effect immediately. STATEMENT This bill, which will be known as the COVID-19 Vaccine Requirement Prohibition Act for Higher Education, prohibits an institution of higher education from requiring a student to receive a vaccination against COVID-19, or presenting evidence of such vaccination, as a condition of: enrollment in the institution; or participation in any program sponsored by the institution.
