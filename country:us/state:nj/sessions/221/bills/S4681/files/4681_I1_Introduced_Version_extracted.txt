Section 1:
S4681 SENATE, No. 4681 STATE OF NEW JERSEY 221st LEGISLATURE INTRODUCED JUNE 30, 2025 Sponsored by: Senator  JOSEPH A. LAGANA District 38 (Bergen) SYNOPSIS Requires parties in arbitration proceedings to be represented by attorneys in certain circumstances. CURRENT VERSION OF TEXT As introduced. An Act concerning arbitration proceedings and supplementing P.L.1983, c.358 (C.39:6A-24 et seq.). Be It Enacted by the Senate and General Assembly of the State of New Jersey: 1.    a.  A party to an arbitration proceeding concerning a claim for personal injury protection coverage shall be represented by an attorney licensed to practice law in the State. b.    As used in this section, personal injury protection coverage means the same as section 4 of P.L.1972, c.70 (C.39:6A-4). 2.    This act shall take effect on the 90th day next following enactment and shall apply to arbitration proceedings that are initiated on or after that date. STATEMENT This bill requires a party in an arbitration proceeding to be represented by an attorney in certain circumstances. Under the bill, a party to an arbitration proceeding concerning a claim for personal injury protection coverage will be required to be represented by an attorney licensed to practice law in New Jersey.
