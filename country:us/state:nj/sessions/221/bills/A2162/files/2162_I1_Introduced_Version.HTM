Title: A2162 Official Title: Number of Sections: 1 Source: versions - Introduced Version Media Type: text/html ================================================================================ Section 1: A2162 ASSEMBLY, No. 2162 STATE OF NEW JERSEY 221st LEGISLATURE PRE-FILED FOR INTRODUCTION IN THE 2024 SESSION Sponsored by: Assemblyman  WILLIAM F. MOEN, JR. District 5 (Camden and Gloucester) Co-Sponsored by: Assemblywoman Matsikoudis SYNOPSIS Authorizes county vocational school districts to establish electric vehicle certification programs. CURRENT VERSION OF TEXT Introduced Pending Technical Review by Legislative Counsel. An Act concerning the establishment of electric vehicle certification programs and supplementing chapter 54 of Title 18A of the New Jersey Statutes. Be It Enacted by the Senate and General Assembly of the State of New Jersey: 1.    a.  A county vocational school district with an automotive program may enter into a partnership with one or more private entities to develop and establish an electric vehicle certification program.  Under the program, the county vocational school district shall identify needed skills, develop training programs, and train students for careers in one or more electric vehicle-related fields including, but not limited to, automotive technology, engineering and design, and maintenance and repair.  The county vocational school district shall issue an electric vehicle certification to each person who successfully completes the training program. b.    A county vocational school district may review national standards established by various industries in developing an electric vehicle certification program. c.     A county vocational school district may consult with the New Jersey Board of Public Utilities in developing an electric vehicle certification program. 2.    This act shall take effect immediately. STATEMENT This bill authorizes a county vocational school district with an automotive program to enter into a partnership with one or more private entities to develop and establish an electric vehicle certification program.  Under the program, the county vocational school district will identify needed skills, develop training programs, and train students for careers in one or more electric vehicle-related fields including, but not limited to, automotive technology, engineering and design, and maintenance and repair.  Under the bill, the county vocational school district will issue an electric vehicle certification to each person who successfully completes the training program. The bill provides that a county vocational school district may review national standards established by various industries, and consult with the New Jersey Board of Public Utilities, in developing an electric vehicle certification program. ================================================================================ Raw Text: A2162 ASSEMBLY, No. 2162 STATE OF NEW JERSEY 221st LEGISLATURE PRE-FILED FOR INTRODUCTION IN THE 2024 SESSION Sponsored by: Assemblyman  WILLIAM F.
