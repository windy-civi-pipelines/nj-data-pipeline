SYNOPSIS

     Requires NJTA and SJTA to display amount of tolls paid in lanes used for electronic toll collection system, commonly known as E-ZPass.

 

CURRENT VERSION OF TEXT

     As introduced.

     3.    (New section) Notwithstanding any law, rule, or regulation to the contrary, the New Jersey Turnpike Authority shall make visible on an electronic display in every lane of every toll plaza of the electronic toll collection system, subject to sections 6 through 10 of P.L.1997, c.59 (C.27:23-34.1 through C.27:23-34.5), of any toll facility operated by the authority, the toll amount paid by a customer with an account for the electronic toll collection system, commonly known as E-ZPass, when passing through the toll plaza; provided, however, that this requirement shall not apply to any toll plaza at which tolls are collected through passage under a high speed gantry.

STATEMENT

 

     This bill requires the New Jersey Turnpike Authority and the South Jersey Transportation Authority (authorities) to make visible, on an electronic display in every lane of every toll plaza of the electronic toll collection system, commonly known as E-ZPass, on the States three toll roads, the New Jersey Turnpike, the Garden State Parkway, and the Atlantic City Expressway, the toll amount paid by a customer with an E-ZPass account when passing through a toll plaza.  This requirement does not apply to any toll plaza at which tolls are collected through passage under a high speed gantry.

     Under the bill, electronic toll collection system is defined as the electronic system employed or utilized by the authorities to register and collect the toll required to be paid for a vehicle entering a toll plaza owned or operated by, or upon the behalf of, the authorities.
