SYNOPSIS

     Requires Medicaid reimbursement of mental health rehabilitation services provided via clubhouse program.

 

CURRENT VERSION OF TEXT

     As reported by the Senate Budget and Appropriations Committee on June 26, 2024, with amendments.

  


An Act concerning Medicaid reimbursement for clubhouse mental health rehabilitation services and amending P.L.1968, c.413.

 

     Be It Enacted by the Senate and General Assembly of the State of New Jersey:
