Section 1:
S1000 2R [Second Reprint] SENATE, No. 1000 STATE OF NEW JERSEY 221st LEGISLATURE PRE-FILED FOR INTRODUCTION IN THE 2024 SESSION Sponsored by: Senator  LINDA R. GREENSTEIN District 14 (Mercer and Middlesex) Senator  PARKER SPACE District 24 (Morris, Sussex and Warren) Co-Sponsored by: Senators Pou, O'Scanlon, Diegnan, A.M.Bucco, Cruz-Perez, Ruiz and Timberlake SYNOPSIS Requires MVC to place designation on motor vehicles registration information indicating registrant is deaf or hard of hearing. CURRENT VERSION OF TEXT As reported by the Senate Budget and Appropriations Committee on February 3, 2025, with amendments. An Act concerning a designation on motor vehicle registration information indicating the registrant is 2deaf or2 1[deaf] hard of hearing1 and supplementing chapter 3 of Title 39 of the Revised Statutes. Be It Enacted by the Senate and General Assembly of the State of New Jersey: 1.    Upon application by any person with a hearing loss 1[of a] as demonstrated by the1 pure tone average of 1500, 1,000, and 2,000 Hertz, of1 41 dB or greater, 1in the better ear, aided or unaided,1 verified by an otorhinolaryngologist (ENT) or an audiologist clinically certified by the American Speech-Language-Hearing Association 1or licensed by the State of New Jersey1, the Chief Administrator of the New Jersey Motor Vehicle Commission shall add a designation to the applicants motor vehicle registration information bearing the international symbol for deafness or a numerical code designating deafness.  The deafness designation shall only appear when a law enforcement officer accesses the motor vehicle's license plate information through the commissions online interface with law enforcement agencies and shall only be used to alert the law enforcement officer that the person is deaf or hard of hearing. 2.    This act shall take effect on the 180th day after enactment and shall apply to motor vehicle registrations issued or renewed on or after this date.
