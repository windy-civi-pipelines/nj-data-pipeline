STATEMENT

 

     This bill supplements the Fiscal Year 2025 appropriations act to make an appropriation of $100,000 from the General Fund to the Department of Environmental Protection to remove and dispose of bird carcasses including for such activities by local governments, including retroactive reimbursements therefor.  The funds may also be disbursed as grants to local governments for the same purpose.

     These funds would support the Division of Fish and Wildlifes efforts to remove contaminated bird carcasses from public environments, in response to the 2025 outbreak of highly pathogenic avian influenza.
