Title: A4157 Official Title: Number of Sections: 1 Source: versions - Introduced Version Media Type: text/html ================================================================================ Section 1: A4157 ASSEMBLY, No. 4157 STATE OF NEW JERSEY 221st LEGISLATURE INTRODUCED APRIL 4, 2024 Sponsored by: Assemblywoman  DAWN FANTASIA District 24 (Morris, Sussex and Warren) Assemblyman  MICHAEL INGANAMORT District 24 (Morris, Sussex and Warren) Assemblyman  ROBERT AUTH District 39 (Bergen) Co-Sponsored by: Assemblyman Azzariti Jr. SYNOPSIS Requires AG to create voting fraud task force. CURRENT VERSION OF TEXT As introduced. An Act concerning voting fraud and supplementing Title 52 of the Revised Statutes. Be It Enacted by the Senate and General Assembly of the State of New Jersey: 1.    The Attorney General shall create a voting fraud task force in the Division of Criminal Justice, Specialized Crimes Bureau to investigate and prosecute crimes involving voting fraud. The task force shall be comprised of members of State, county, and municipal law enforcement agencies, as determined by the Attorney General, and shall utilize a coordinated law enforcement strategy to address voting fraud in this State. 2.    This act shall take effect on the first day of the sixth month next following enactment. STATEMENT This bill requires the Attorney General to create a voting fraud task force in the Division of Criminal Justice, Specialized Crimes Bureau.  The purpose of the task force is to investigate and prosecute crimes involving voting fraud. Under the bill, the task force is to be comprised of members of State, county, and municipal law enforcement agencies, as determined by the Attorney General, and is to utilize a coordinated law enforcement strategy to address voting fraud in this State. ================================================================================ Raw Text: A4157 ASSEMBLY, No. 4157 STATE OF NEW JERSEY 221st LEGISLATURE INTRODUCED APRIL 4, 2024 Sponsored by: Assemblywoman  DAWN FANTASIA District 24 (Morris, Sussex and Warren) Assemblyman  MICHAEL INGANAMORT District 24 (Morris, Sussex and Warren) Assemblyman  ROBERT AUTH District 39 (Bergen) Co-Sponsored by: Assemblyman Azzariti Jr. SYNOPSIS Requires AG to create voting fraud task force.
