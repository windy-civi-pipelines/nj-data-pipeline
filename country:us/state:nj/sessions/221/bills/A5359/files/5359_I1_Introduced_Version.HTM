SYNOPSIS

     Establishes annual fee for sports wagering licensees; directs funds from such fees to Council on Compulsive Gambling and gambling addiction treatment programs.

     2.    This act shall take effect January 1 next following the date of enactment.

 

 

STATEMENT

 

     Under current law, the Division of Gaming Enforcement is authorized to establish by regulations the license issuance and renewal fees for sports wagering licenses.  Such fees are currently set at $100,000 annually, with 50 percent of these fees being dedicated to gambling addiction treatment programs.

     This bill establishes an annual fee to be paid by sports wagering licensees, in addition to license issuance and annual renewal fees.  The bill establishes this fee as $250,000, with $140,000 being allocated to the Council on Compulsive Gambling of New Jersey and the remaining $110,000 being used for other compulsive gambling treatment programs in this State.
