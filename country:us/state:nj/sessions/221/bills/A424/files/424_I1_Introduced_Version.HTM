Title: A424 Official Title: Number of Sections: 1 Source: versions - Introduced Version Media Type: text/html ================================================================================ Section 1: A424 ASSEMBLY, No. 424 STATE OF NEW JERSEY 221st LEGISLATURE PRE-FILED FOR INTRODUCTION IN THE 2024 SESSION Sponsored by: Assemblyman  ROBERT AUTH District 39 (Bergen and Passaic) Co-Sponsored by: Assemblywoman Flynn and Assemblyman Greenwald SYNOPSIS Retains organ donor designation upon renewal and issuance of certain documents. CURRENT VERSION OF TEXT Introduced Pending Technical Review by Legislative Counsel. An Act concerning organ donor designation and supplementing Title 39 of the Revised Statutes. Be It Enacted by the Senate and General Assembly of the State of New Jersey: 1.    Notwithstanding any law, rule, regulation, or order to the contrary, a person who is designated as an organ donor on the persons examination permit, special learners permit, motorcycle-only examination permit, commercial learners permit, motorized bicycle license, basic drivers license, probationary drivers license, commercial driver license, or identification card, pursuant to section 1 of P.L.1978, c.181 (C.39:3-12.2), shall remain designated as an organ donor upon the renewal of such permit, license, or identification card, and upon the subsequent issuance of a license or identification card to the person, unless the person removes the designation.  The Chief Administrator of the New Jersey Motor Vehicle Commission shall provide the opportunity to remove the designation upon renewal of a permit, license, or identification card and upon the subsequent issuance of a license or identification card to the person. 2.    This act shall take effect on the first day of the fourth month after enactment, but the Chief Administrator of the New Jersey Motor Vehicle Commission may take such administrative action in advance of the effective date as may be necessary. STATEMENT This bill provides that an organ donor designation on a drivers permit, drivers license, or identification card is to remain upon the renewal of the permit, license, or identification card, and upon the subsequent issuance of a license or identification card to the person.  The Chief Administrator of the New Jersey Motor Vehicle Commission is required to provide an opportunity to remove the organ donor designation upon renewal of the permit, license, or identification card and subsequent issuance of a license or identification card to the person.  Currently, a person is required to confirm the persons organ donor designation at each license or identification card renewal. ================================================================================ Raw Text: A424 ASSEMBLY, No. 424 STATE OF NEW JERSEY 221st LEGISLATURE PRE-FILED FOR INTRODUCTION IN THE 2024 SESSION Sponsored by: Assemblyman  ROBERT AUTH District 39 (Bergen and Passaic) Co-Sponsored by: Assemblywoman Flynn and Assemblyman Greenwald SYNOPSIS Retains organ donor designation upon renewal and issuance of certain documents.
