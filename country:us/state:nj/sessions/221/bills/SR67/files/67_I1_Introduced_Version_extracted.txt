Section 1:
SR67 SENATE RESOLUTION No. 67 STATE OF NEW JERSEY 221st LEGISLATURE INTRODUCED FEBRUARY 5, 2024 Sponsored by: Senator  SHIRLEY K. TURNER District 15 (Hunterdon and Mercer) SYNOPSIS Memorializes Congress to provide Medicare coverage for eyeglasses, hearing aids, and dentures. CURRENT VERSION OF TEXT As introduced. A Senate Resolution memorializing Congress to provide Medicare coverage for eyeglasses, hearing aids, and dentures. Whereas, A large portion of the population in the United States wears eyeglasses, dentures, or hearing aids; with the aging of the population, a growing number of older Americans are likely to have greater health care needs, and, in particular, will need to purchase eyeglasses, hearing aids, or dentures; and Whereas, Despite the availability of Medicare and Medicaid, many older Americans still are unable to meet their health care needs for eyeglasses, hearing aids, and dentures; and Whereas, As people age, eyesight diminishes and the loss of focusing ability that accompanies aging makes it difficult to read; nearly 100% of the population 55 years of age or older is affected by this condition, known as presbyopia, which can be remedied by wearing reading eyeglasses or eyeglasses with bifocal lenses; and Whereas, Approximately 42% of the elderly Medicare population have moderate or profound hearing loss and the price of a hearing aid, which can range from hundreds to thousands of dollars for a single device, steadily increases every year; and Whereas, Most senior citizens of this country suffer from periodontitis, the inflammation of the gums and bones supporting the teeth, and many have lost their teeth and need to repair, or be fitted with, dentures; and Whereas, Medicare coverage does not cover the cost of eyeglasses, hearing aids, or dentures; and Whereas, Senior citizens confront increasing medical, hospital, and other health care-related expenses and often have to meet these costs on a fixed income; and Whereas, Coverage of the cost of eyeglasses, hearing aids, and dentures would provide access to eyeglasses, hearing aids, and dentures for elderly Americans and help improve their quality of life, restore their dignity, and alleviate the financial burden many senior citizens face when they need assistive devices to remain vibrant members of their community; now, therefore, Be It Resolved by the Senate of the State of New Jersey: 1.    This House respectfully memorializes the United States Congress to enact legislation to provide Medicare coverage for eyeglasses, hearing aids, and dentures. 2.    Copies of this resolution, as filed with the Secretary of State, shall be transmitted by the Secretary of the Senate to the Majority and Minority Leaders of the United States Senate, the Speaker and Minority Leader of the United States House of Representatives, and every member of the United States Congress elected from this State. STATEMENT This resolution respectfully memorializes the United States Congress to enact legislation to provide Medicare coverage for eyeglasses, hearing aids, and dentures.
