Section 1:
S3723 SENATE, No. 3723 STATE OF NEW JERSEY 221st LEGISLATURE INTRODUCED OCTOBER 7, 2024 Sponsored by: Senator  VIN GOPAL District 11 (Monmouth) Senator  ANGELA V. MCKNIGHT District 31 (Hudson) SYNOPSIS Prohibits public institutions of higher education from increasing resident undergraduate tuition by more than two percent over prior academic year. CURRENT VERSION OF TEXT As introduced. An Act concerning tuition rates at public institutions of higher education and supplementing chapter 62 of Title 18A of the New Jersey Statutes. Be It Enacted by the Senate and General Assembly of the State of New Jersey: 1.    Notwithstanding the provisions of any law, rule, or regulation to the contrary, a public institution of higher education shall not increase the institutions resident undergraduate tuition rates for any academic year by more than two percent over the institutions resident undergraduate tuition rates for the prior academic year. 2.    This act shall take effect immediately and shall first apply to the first full academic year following the date of enactment. STATEMENT This bill prohibits a public institution of higher education from increasing the institutions resident undergraduate tuition rates for any academic year by more than two percent over the institutions resident undergraduate tuition rates for the prior academic year.
