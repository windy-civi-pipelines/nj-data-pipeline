Section 1:
A2597 ASSEMBLY, No. 2597 STATE OF NEW JERSEY 221st LEGISLATURE PRE-FILED FOR INTRODUCTION IN THE 2024 SESSION Sponsored by: Assemblyman  ROBERT J. KARABINCHAK District 18 (Middlesex) Assemblywoman  VERLINA REYNOLDS-JACKSON District 15 (Hunterdon and Mercer) SYNOPSIS Prohibits MVC from charging service fees for online services. CURRENT VERSION OF TEXT Introduced Pending Technical Review by Legislative Counsel. An Act concerning fees charged by the New Jersey Motor Vehicle Commission and supplementing Title 39 of the Revised Statutes. Be It Enacted by the Senate and General Assembly of the State of New Jersey: 1.    Notwithstanding any provision of law, rule, or regulation to the contrary, the New Jersey Motor Vehicle Commission shall not charge any additional fee for any service provided by the commission through the commissions website that is not charged for the same service when that service is provided at a commission agency or regional service center. 2.    This act shall take effect immediately. STATEMENT This bill prohibits the New Jersey Motor Vehicle Commission (MVC) from charging any additional fee for any service provided through the MVC website that is not charged for the same service when that service is provided at an MVC agency or regional service center. In 2016, the MVC temporarily eliminated the service fee for MVC services provided through online transactions.  On April 24, 2019, the MVC reinstated the online service fee.  This bill permanently eliminates the online service fees that are not charged on services provided in-person at MVC agencies or regional service centers.
