SYNOPSIS

     Requires NJEDA to establish loan program to assist beginning farmers in financing capital purchases.

 

CURRENT VERSION OF TEXT

     As reported by the Assembly Commerce, Economic Development and Agriculture Committee on June 3, 2024, with amendments.
