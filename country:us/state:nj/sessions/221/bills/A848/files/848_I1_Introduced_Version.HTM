STATEMENT

 

      This bill provides a supplemental appropriation of $35 million from the General Fund to the Urban and Rural Centers Unsafe Buildings Demolition Revolving Loan Fund.  This fund was established pursuant to P.L.1997, c.125 to assist municipalities with building demolition and disposal projects.
