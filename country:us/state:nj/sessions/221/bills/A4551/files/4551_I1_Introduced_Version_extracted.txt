Section 1:
A4551 ASSEMBLY, No. 4551 STATE OF NEW JERSEY 221st LEGISLATURE INTRODUCED JUNE 13, 2024 Sponsored by: Assemblyman  GREGORY P. MCGUCKIN District 10 (Monmouth and Ocean) SYNOPSIS Requires applications to decline receipt of mail-in ballots for future elections be available at polling places on election day. CURRENT VERSION OF TEXT As introduced. An Act concerning the availability of applications to decline receipt of mail-in ballots for future elections at polling places and supplementing P.L.2009, c.79 (C.19:63-1 et seq.). Be It Enacted by the Senate and General Assembly of the State of New Jersey: 1.  The County Clerk shall cause to be made available at each polling place on the day of any election copies of applications to decline the receipt of mail-in ballots for future elections. 2.  This act shall take effect on the January 1 next following the date of enactment. STATEMENT This bill requires applications to decline the receipt of mail-in ballots for future elections be available at polling places on election day.  Under the bill, the County Clerk would make available at each polling place on the day of any election copies of applications to decline the receipt of mail-in ballots for future elections.
