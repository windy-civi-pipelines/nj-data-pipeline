Section 1:
S3976 1R [First Reprint] SENATE, No. 3976 STATE OF NEW JERSEY 221st LEGISLATURE INTRODUCED DECEMBER 16, 2024 Sponsored by: Senator  ROBERT W. SINGER District 30 (Monmouth and Ocean) Senator  ANTHONY M. BUCCO District 25 (Morris and Passaic) SYNOPSIS Grants priority course registration to certain student parents attending institutions of higher education. CURRENT VERSION OF TEXT As reported by the Senate Higher Education Committee on June 12, 2025, with amendments. An Act concerning priority registration at institutions of higher education and supplementing chapter 62 of Title 18A of the New Jersey Statutes. Be It Enacted by the Senate and General Assembly of the State of New Jersey: 1. a. 1[A] An undergraduate1 student enrolled in an institution of higher education that provides priority registration for 1any other undergraduate1 students shall be granted priority in registering for courses at the institution provided that the student is: (1) the parent or legal guardian of a child who is under 18 years of age; and (2) a resident of the State. b. Priority course registration shall occur using the same procedures used by other segments of the student population that are offered priority registration by the institution. c. Prior to the registration period for each academic semester, an institution shall provide notice to students who may be eligible for priority registration pursuant to this section. 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
