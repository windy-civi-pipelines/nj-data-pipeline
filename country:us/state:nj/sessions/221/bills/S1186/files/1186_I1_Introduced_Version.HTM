Title: S1186 Official Title: Number of Sections: 1 Source: versions - Introduced Version Media Type: text/html ================================================================================ Section 1: S1186 SENATE, No. 1186 STATE OF NEW JERSEY 221st LEGISLATURE PRE-FILED FOR INTRODUCTION IN THE 2024 SESSION Sponsored by: Senator  RAJ MUKHERJI District 32 (Hudson) SYNOPSIS Prohibits carriers from offering dental plans with dental benefit waiting periods. CURRENT VERSION OF TEXT Introduced Pending Technical Review by Legislative Counsel. An Act concerning dental benefit waiting periods and supplementing P.L.2014, c.70 (C26:2S-26 et seq.). Be It Enacted by the Senate and General Assembly of the State of New Jersey: 1.    a.  Notwithstanding any law, rule, or regulation to the contrary, a carrier issuing, executing, or renewing a dental plan in this State shall be prohibited from providing a plan to a covered person with a dental benefit waiting period. b.    For the purposes of this section, dental benefit waiting period means a provision within a dental plan that delays the eligibility of a covered person to receive certain covered services under the plan. 2.    This act shall take effect on the 30th day next following enactment, and shall apply to dental plans issued, executed, or renewed on or after that date. STATEMENT This bill prohibits carriers from offering dental plans with a dental benefit waiting period. Under the bill, a carrier issuing, executing, or renewing a dental plan in this State will be prohibited from providing a plan to a covered person with a dental benefit waiting period. For the purposes of this bill, dental benefit waiting period means a provision within a dental plan that delays the eligibility of a covered person to receive certain covered services under the plan. ================================================================================ Raw Text: S1186 SENATE, No. 1186 STATE OF NEW JERSEY 221st LEGISLATURE PRE-FILED FOR INTRODUCTION IN THE 2024 SESSION Sponsored by: Senator  RAJ MUKHERJI District 32 (Hudson) SYNOPSIS Prohibits carriers from offering dental plans with dental benefit waiting periods.
