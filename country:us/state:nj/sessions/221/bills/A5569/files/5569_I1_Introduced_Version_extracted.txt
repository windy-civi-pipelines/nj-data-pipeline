Section 1:
A5569 ASSEMBLY, No. 5569 STATE OF NEW JERSEY 221st LEGISLATURE INTRODUCED APRIL 10, 2025 Sponsored by: Assemblyman  CHRISTOPHER P. DEPHILLIPS District 40 (Bergen, Essex and Passaic) SYNOPSIS Requires NJT to rescind certain fare increases. CURRENT VERSION OF TEXT As introduced. An Act concerning New Jersey Transit Corporation fare increases. Be It Enacted by the Senate and General Assembly of the State of New Jersey: 1.    The New Jersey Transit Corporation shall rescind the fare increase of three percent, effective July 1, 2025, approved and authorized by the board pursuant to the April 15, 2024 board meeting minutes. 2.    This act shall take effect immediately. STATEMENT This bill requires the New Jersey Transit Corporation (corporation) to rescind the fare increase of three percent, effective July 1, 2025, approved and authorized by the corporations board pursuant to the April 15, 2024 board meeting minutes.
