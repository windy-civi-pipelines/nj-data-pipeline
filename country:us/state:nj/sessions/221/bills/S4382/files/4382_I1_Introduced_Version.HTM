Title: S4382 Official Title: Number of Sections: 1 Source: versions - Introduced Version Media Type: text/html ================================================================================ Section 1: S4382 SENATE, No. 4382 STATE OF NEW JERSEY 221st LEGISLATURE INTRODUCED MAY 19, 2025 Sponsored by: Senator  PATRICK J. DIEGNAN, JR. District 18 (Middlesex) Co-Sponsored by: Senator Burgess SYNOPSIS Prohibits certain animal adoption centers from denying applicant for adoption of dog or cat solely due to applicants age. CURRENT VERSION OF TEXT As introduced. An Act concerning the adoption of dogs and cats and supplementing Title 4 of the Revised Statutes. Be It Enacted by the Senate and General Assembly of the State of New Jersey: 1.  a.  As used in this section: Animal rescue organization means the same as the term is defined in section 1 of P.L.1941, c.151 (C.4:19-15.1). Foster home means the same as the term is defined in section 1 of P.L.1941, c.151 (C.4:19-15.1). Pound means the same as the term is defined in section 1 of P.L.1941, c.151 (C.4:19-15.1). Shelter means the same as the term is defined in section 1 of P.L.1941, c.151 (C.4:19-15.1). b.  Notwithstanding the provisions of any law, regulation, or rule to the contrary, no animal rescue organization, shelter, pound, or foster home shall deny an applicant for the adoption of a dog or cat solely on the basis of the applicants age. c.  Every animal rescue organization, shelter, pound, or foster home shall ensure that the animal rescue organization, shelter, pound, or foster homes adoption eligibility criteria for the adoption of a dog or cat is in compliance with the requirements of subsection b. of this section. 2.  This act shall take effect immediately. STATEMENT This bill would prohibit animal rescue organizations, shelters, pounds, and foster homes from denying an applicant for the adoption of a dog or cat solely on the basis of the applicants age.  The bill would also require every animal rescue organization, shelter, pound, or foster home to ensure that the animal rescue organization, shelter, pound, or foster homes adoption eligibility criteria for the adoption of a dog or cat is in compliance with the bills requirements. ================================================================================ Raw Text: S4382 SENATE, No. 4382 STATE OF NEW JERSEY 221st LEGISLATURE INTRODUCED MAY 19, 2025 Sponsored by: Senator  PATRICK J.
