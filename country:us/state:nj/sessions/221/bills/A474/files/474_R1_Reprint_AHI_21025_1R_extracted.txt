A474 1R [First Reprint] ASSEMBLY, No. 474 STATE OF NEW JERSEY 221st LEGISLATURE PRE-FILED FOR INTRODUCTION IN THE 2024 SESSION Sponsored by: Assemblyman  JOE DANIELSEN District 17 (Middlesex and Somerset) SYNOPSIS Makes supplemental appropriation of $500,000 from General Fund to Rutgers University  New Brunswick for Nurture thru Nature program. ($500,000) 2.  This act shall take effect immediately.
