Section 1:
A5945 ASSEMBLY, No. 5945 STATE OF NEW JERSEY 221st LEGISLATURE INTRODUCED JULY 24, 2025 Sponsored by: Assemblyman  ERIK K. SIMONSEN District 1 (Atlantic, Cape May and Cumberland) SYNOPSIS Establishes maximum size for lands leased as shellfisheries areas along the Atlantic Coast by Shellfisheries Council. CURRENT VERSION OF TEXT As introduced. An Act concerning lands leased as shellfisheries and amending R.S.50:1-27. Be It Enacted by the Senate and General Assembly of the State of New Jersey: 1.    R.S.50:1-27 is amended to read as follows: 50:1-27.      a.  The council, with the approval of the commissioner, shall fix the term for which leases may be granted, the rental to be paid, the maximum size of any single ground to be leased, and the total acreage which may be leased to any person or persons. b.  The council shall set the maximum size of any single ground to be leased on the Atlantic coast at 50 acres. (cf: P.L.2007, c.338, s.13) 2.  This act shall take effect immediately. STATEMENT This bill establishes the maximum size for any single ground that is leased as a shellfishery located on the Atlantic coast at 50 acres.  Under current law, the Atlantic Coast is the tidal waters of the State, except those of the Delaware River, Delaware Bay, and their tributaries.
