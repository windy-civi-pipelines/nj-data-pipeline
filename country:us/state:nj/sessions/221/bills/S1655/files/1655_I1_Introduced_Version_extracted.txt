Section 1:
S1655 SENATE, No. 1655 STATE OF NEW JERSEY 221st LEGISLATURE PRE-FILED FOR INTRODUCTION IN THE 2024 SESSION Sponsored by: Senator  DECLAN J. O'SCANLON, JR. District 13 (Monmouth) Senator  MICHAEL L. TESTA, JR. District 1 (Atlantic, Cape May and Cumberland) Co-Sponsored by: Senators Corrado and Space SYNOPSIS Prohibits bill certified for fiscal note or estimate from being released from committee without fiscal note or estimate. CURRENT VERSION OF TEXT Introduced Pending Technical Review by Legislative Counsel. An Act prohibiting bills certified for a fiscal note or fiscal estimate from being released from committee without a fiscal note or fiscal estimate, and amending P.L1980, c.67. Be It Enacted by the Senate and General Assembly of the State of New Jersey: 1.  Section 5 of P.L.1980, c.67 (C.52:13B-10) is amended to read as follows: 5.    a.  When the Legislative Budget and Finance Officer has a complete fiscal note or legislative fiscal estimate, the Officer shall transmit an electronic copy of the fiscal note or legislative fiscal estimate to the sponsor whose name first appears on the bill with a notice that the sponsor may object to the fiscal note or legislative fiscal estimate within three business days after the transmittal of the electronic copy. b.    If the Legislative Budget and Finance Officer has not received a complete fiscal note or legislative fiscal estimate for a bill that has been certified for such note or estimate, the bill shall not be released from committee unless and until the fiscal note or legislative fiscal estimate is complete. (cf:  P.L.2007, c.151, s.5) 2.  This act shall take effect on the first day of the third month following enactment. STATEMENT This bill prohibits a bill that that has been certified for a fiscal note or legislative fiscal estimate from being released from committee unless and until the fiscal note or legislative fiscal estimate is complete.
