Title: SJR119 Official Title: Number of Sections: 1 Source: versions - Introduced Version Media Type: text/html ================================================================================ Section 1: SJR119 SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION No. 119 STATE OF NEW JERSEY 221st LEGISLATURE INTRODUCED JUNE 3, 2024 Sponsored by: Senator  ROBERT W. SINGER District 30 (Monmouth and Ocean) SYNOPSIS Changes Jewish Heritage Month observed in April of each year to Jewish American Heritage Month to be observed in May of each year. CURRENT VERSION OF TEXT As introduced. A Joint Resolution changing Jewish Heritage Month, observed in April of each year in this State, to Jewish American Heritage Month to be observed in May of each year and amending P.L.2008, J.R.1. Be It Resolved by the Senate and General Assembly of the State of New Jersey: 1.  Section 1 of P.L.2008, J.R.1 (C.36:2-109) is amended to read as follows: 1.    The month of [April] May of each year is hereby designated as "Jewish American Heritage Month" in the State of New Jersey to recognize and honor the diverse contributions and achievements Jewish Americans have made to the development of our State and the nation. (cf: P.L.2008, J.R.1, s.1) 2.  This joint resolution shall take effect immediately. STATEMENT Under current law, Jewish Heritage Month is observed during the month of April in this State to honor the contributions and achievements Jewish Americans have made to this State and to the nation.  On April 20, 2006, President George W. Bush proclaimed that May would be Jewish American Heritage Month.  This resolution amends current law to be consistent with the national observance of Jewish American Heritage Month during the month of May of each year. ================================================================================ Raw Text: SJR119 SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION No. 119 STATE OF NEW JERSEY 221st LEGISLATURE INTRODUCED JUNE 3, 2024 Sponsored by: Senator  ROBERT W.
