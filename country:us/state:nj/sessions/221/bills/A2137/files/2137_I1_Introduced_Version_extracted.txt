Section 1:
A2137 ASSEMBLY, No. 2137 STATE OF NEW JERSEY 221st LEGISLATURE PRE-FILED FOR INTRODUCTION IN THE 2024 SESSION Sponsored by: Assemblyman  WILLIAM F. MOEN, JR. District 5 (Camden and Gloucester) SYNOPSIS Directs MVC to implement and offer to public wireless telephone application technology that allows person to add digital version of certain identification to wireless telephone. CURRENT VERSION OF TEXT Introduced Pending Technical Review by Legislative Counsel. An Act concerning digital drivers licenses and non-driver identification and supplementing Title 39 of the Revised Statutes. Be It Enacted by the Senate and General Assembly of the State of New Jersey: 1.    The New Jersey Motor Vehicle Commission shall implement and offer to the public a wireless telephone application technology that allows a person to add a digital version of the persons New Jersey drivers license or non-driver identification card to a wireless telephone. The wireless telephone application technology shall meet standards established by the International Organization for Standardization. 2.    The Chief Administrator of the New Jersey Motor Vehicle Commission shall adopt rules and regulations, pursuant to the provisions of the Administrative Procedure Act, P.L.1968, c.410 (C.52:14B-1 et seq.), to effectuate the purpose of this act. 3.    This act shall take effect immediately. STATEMENT This bill directs the New Jersey Motor Vehicle Commission to implement and offer to the public a wireless telephone application technology, which meets standards established by the International Organization for Standardization, that allows a person to add a digital version of the persons New Jersey drivers license or non-driver identification card to a wireless telephone.
