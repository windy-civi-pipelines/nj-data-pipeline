STATEMENT

 

     The Governors Fiscal Year 2022 budget recommendations include the enactment of legislation to require the State Health Benefits Commission and School Employees Health Benefit Commission to implement a referenced based pricing program for certain elective medical procedures and a bundled payment program.  This bill authorizes the commissions to implement both programs.
