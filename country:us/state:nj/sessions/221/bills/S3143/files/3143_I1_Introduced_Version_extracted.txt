Section 1:
S3143 SENATE, No. 3143 STATE OF NEW JERSEY 221st LEGISLATURE INTRODUCED MAY 6, 2024 Sponsored by: Senator  ROBERT W. SINGER District 30 (Monmouth and Ocean) Senator  ANGELA V. MCKNIGHT District 31 (Hudson) Co-Sponsored by: Senator Greenstein SYNOPSIS Requires hospitals and nursing homes to provide access to certain interpreter services for deaf and hard of hearing. CURRENT VERSION OF TEXT As introduced. An Act concerning interpreter services and supplementing Title 26 of the Revised Statutes. Be It Enacted by the Senate and General Assembly of the State of New Jersey: 1.  a.  Each hospital and nursing home licensed pursuant to P.L.1971, c.136 (C.26:2H-1 et seq.) shall make interpreter services for the deaf and hard of hearing available to patients 24 hours per day, seven days per week.  Interpreter services shall include, at minimum, interpreter services provided through electronic means including, but not limited to, video remote interpreting services and artificial intelligence-assisted interpreting services, as approved by the Commissioner of Health. 2.  This act shall take effect 180 days after the date of enactment. STATEMENT This bill requires each hospital and nursing home licensed pursuant to P.L.1971, c.136 (C.26:2H-1 et seq.) to make interpreter services for the deaf and hard of hearing available to patients 24 hours per day, seven days per week.  Interpreter services will include, at minimum, interpreter services provided through electronic means including, but not limited to, video remote interpreting services and artificial intelligence-assisted interpreting services, as approved by the Commissioner of Health.
