Section 1:
S3264 1R [First Reprint] SENATE, No. 3264 STATE OF NEW JERSEY 221st LEGISLATURE INTRODUCED MAY 16, 2024 Sponsored by: Senator  GORDON M. JOHNSON District 37 (Bergen) Senator  NILSA I. CRUZ-PEREZ District 5 (Camden and Gloucester) Co-Sponsored by: Senator Greenstein SYNOPSIS Makes supplemental appropriation of $500,000 to DCA for NJ State Veterans Chamber of Commerce for veteran economic development and outreach and skills development. CURRENT VERSION OF TEXT As reported by the Senate Military and Veterans' Affairs Committee on October 21, 2024, with amendments. A Supplement to the annual appropriations act for the fiscal year ending 1[June 30, 2024, P.L.2023, c.74] June 30, 2025, P.L.2024, c.221. Be It Enacted by the Senate and the General Assembly of the State of New Jersey: 1. In addition to the amounts appropriated under 1[P.L.2023, c.74] P.L.2024, c.221, the annual appropriations act for the fiscal year ending 1[June 30, 2024] June 30, 20251, there is appropriated from the General Fund the following amount for the purpose specified: 22 DEPARTMENT OF COMMUNITY AFFAIRS 50 Economic Planning, Development, and Security 55 Social Services Programs GRANTS-IN-AID 05-8050  Community Resources............................................. $500,000 Total Grants-in-Aid Appropriation, Social Services Programs ................................... $500,000 Grants-in-Aid: 05    New Jersey State Veterans Chamber of Commerce ($500,000) The amount hereinabove appropriated for New Jersey State Veterans Chamber of Commerce shall be utilized for programs or resources operated or authorized by the New Jersey State Veterans Chamber of Commerce that aim to address veteran economic development and outreach and skills development for veterans. 2.  This act shall take effect immediately.
