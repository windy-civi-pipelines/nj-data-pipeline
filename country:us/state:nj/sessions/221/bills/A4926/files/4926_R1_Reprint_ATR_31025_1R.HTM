SYNOPSIS

     Directs DEP and DOT to establish Wildlife Corridor Action Plan; appropriates $90,000.

 

CURRENT VERSION OF TEXT

     As reported by the Assembly Transportation and Independent Authorities Committee on March 10, 2025, with amendments.

  


An Act concerning the movement of wildlife throughout the State, supplementing Title 23 1[and Title 27]1 of the Revised Statutes, and making an appropriation.
