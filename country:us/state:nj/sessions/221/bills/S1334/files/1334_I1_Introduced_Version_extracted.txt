Section 1:
S1334 SENATE, No. 1334 STATE OF NEW JERSEY 221st LEGISLATURE PRE-FILED FOR INTRODUCTION IN THE 2024 SESSION Sponsored by: Senator  MICHAEL L. TESTA, JR. District 1 (Atlantic, Cape May and Cumberland) Senator  KRISTIN M. CORRADO District 40 (Bergen, Essex and Passaic) Co-Sponsored by: Senator Schepisi SYNOPSIS Requires MVC to provide certain vehicle and licensing services in each county in State. CURRENT VERSION OF TEXT Introduced Pending Technical Review by Legislative Counsel. An Act requiring the New Jersey Motor Vehicle Commission to provide vehicle and licensing services in each county in the State and supplementing Title 39 of the Revised Statutes. Be It Enacted by the Senate and General Assembly of the State of New Jersey: 1.    Notwithstanding the provision of any law, rule, or regulation to the contrary, the New Jersey Motor Vehicle Commission shall operate a vehicle agency and a licensing agency in each county in the State, or shall operate an agency in each county in the State that provides both vehicle and licensing services. For purposes of this section: Licensing agency shall mean a commission office that provides licensing and identification services, including, but not limited to, the issuance of basic drivers licenses, examination permits, special learners permits, probationary drivers licenses, and identification cards; Vehicle agency shall mean a commission office that provides motor vehicle registration services, including, but not limited to, the registration of motor vehicles and the issuance of registration certificates, motor vehicle certificates of ownership, and motor vehicle license plates; and Vehicle and licensing services shall include, but not be limited to, the issuance of basic drivers licenses, examination permits, special learners permits, probationary drivers licenses, identification cards, registration certificates, motor vehicle certificates of ownership, and motor vehicle license plates. 2.    This act shall take effect on the first day of the third month after enactment. STATEMENT This bill requires the New Jersey Motor Vehicle Commission to operate a vehicle agency and a licensing agency in each county in the State, or to operate a commission agency in each county in the State that provides both vehicle and licensing services.
