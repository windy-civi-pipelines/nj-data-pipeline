SYNOPSIS

     Permits certain consumers up to five business days to cancel home improvement contracts and up to three business days to cancel certain consumer contracts.

 

CURRENT VERSION OF TEXT

     As reported by the Assembly Consumer Affairs Committee on October 24, 2024, with amendments.
