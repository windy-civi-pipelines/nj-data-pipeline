Section 1:
A5784 ASSEMBLY, No. 5784 STATE OF NEW JERSEY 221st LEGISLATURE INTRODUCED JUNE 16, 2025 Sponsored by: Assemblyman  ROBERT J. KARABINCHAK District 18 (Middlesex) SYNOPSIS Permits county clerk to require electronic submission of certain documents. CURRENT VERSION OF TEXT As introduced. An Act permitting county clerks to require electronic submission of certain documents and supplementing Title 40A of the New Jersey Statutes. Be It Enacted by the Senate and General Assembly of the State of New Jersey: 1.  Notwithstanding any law, rule, or regulation to the contrary, a county clerk may require any document submitted to the county clerks office by a business that employs 10 or more individuals to be submitted electronically to the county clerks office in a form and manner as determined by the county clerk. 2.  This act shall take effect immediately. STATEMENT This bill allows county clerks to require documents submitted to the county clerks office by businesses that employ 10 or more individuals to be submitted electronically to the county clerks office in a form and manner determined by the county clerk.
